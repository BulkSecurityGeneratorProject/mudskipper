Welcome to the Mudskipper project.

My latest news is the pre-production release v0.0.1-ALPHA (BabyBoy) tonight of a working JHipster application that keeps online accounts, usernames, emails, and passwords. This application has become my reference JHipster application for learning and also an application that my company CI Wise Inc. will actually use. It will be deployed to Heroku and use a PostgreSQL database. I will be sharing more features and functionality in futures releases and I'll also be describing the future application overall. I have a vision for the application and I'll be sharing that soon.
